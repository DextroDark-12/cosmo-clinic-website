What is Microneedling?

Microneedling is a collagen induction therapy that uses fine, sterile needles to create controlled micro-injuries in the skin, triggering its natural healing response.

These microscopic channels stimulate the production of new collagen and elastin—the structural proteins that keep skin firm, smooth, and youthful. The process also creates pathways for topical serums to penetrate deeper into the skin.

It is one of the most versatile and effective treatments for improving skin texture, reducing the appearance of scars, refining pores, and addressing fine lines. Results develop progressively over several months.

⏱ Duration 45–60 min
✨ Results 2–4 weeks progressive
🌿 Suitable For All skin types
🔄 Sessions 3–6 sessions, monthly

Key Benefits

✦

Collagen Induction

Controlled micro-injuries trigger the skin’s wound-healing cascade, stimulating the production of new collagen and elastin for structural improvement.

✦

Scar Reduction

Acne scars, surgical scars, and stretch marks respond well to microneedling. Over a treatment series, scar depth and appearance are visibly reduced.

✦

Pore Refinement

By stimulating collagen around enlarged pores and improving skin density, microneedling visibly reduces pore size and improves overall texture.

✦

Enhanced Product Absorption

The micro-channels created during treatment allow topical serums to penetrate up to 80% deeper, maximizing the efficacy of active ingredients.

The Procedure

01

Cleanse & Apply Numbing

The skin is thoroughly cleansed, and a topical anesthetic is applied for 20–30 minutes to ensure comfort throughout the procedure.

02

Precision Microneedling

A sterile, single-use needle cartridge is passed over the skin, creating controlled micro-injuries at a depth tailored to your specific concerns and skin type.

03

Serum Infusion & Calming

A customized serum is applied immediately after needling, penetrating deeply through the micro-channels. A soothing mask calms the skin post-treatment.

Treatment Duration 45–60 min per session
Aftercare Avoid makeup for 24 hours. No active skincare for 48 hours. SPF mandatory for 1 week. No exfoliation for 5 days.
