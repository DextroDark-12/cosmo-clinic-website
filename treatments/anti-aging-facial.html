What is the Anti-Aging Facial?

Our Anti-Aging Facial is a comprehensive treatment designed to restore skin vitality, improve texture, and support a more youthful appearance—without aggressive procedures or recovery time.

Using a combination of deep exfoliation, collagen-supporting serums, and targeted hydration, this facial addresses the specific needs of maturing skin. It targets fine lines, uneven texture, dullness, and loss of firmness with evidence-backed techniques tailored to your skin's current condition.

Whether you're looking to maintain your skin's current state or address visible signs of aging, this treatment provides a realistic, sustainable approach to skincare that works with your skin—not against it.

⏱ Duration 60–75 min
✨ Results Immediate glow
🌿 Suitable For All skin types
🔄 Sessions 4–6 for best results

Key Benefits

✦

Improved Texture

Deep exfoliation smooths the skin's surface, reducing the appearance of fine lines and creating a softer, more even texture.

✦

Deep Hydration

Targeted humectants and peptides replenish moisture at the cellular level, restoring plumpness and reducing dryness-related lines.

✦

Brighter Appearance

Antioxidant-rich formulations address dullness and uneven tone, revealing a more luminous, rested-looking complexion.

✦

Collagen Support

Advanced serums containing peptides and growth factors support the skin's natural collagen production for longer-term structural benefits.

The Procedure

01

Assess & Prepare

We begin with a thorough skin assessment to understand your specific concerns—fine lines, texture, hydration levels, and sensitivity. This guides the selection of active ingredients for your session.

02

Exfoliate & Infuse

A gentle yet effective exfoliation removes the outer layer of dead skin cells, followed by the infusion of collagen-supporting peptides and hydrating agents deep into the skin through targeted application.

03

Protect & Seal

A final layer of antioxidant-rich serums and SPF protection seals the treatment, locking in benefits and shielding the skin from environmental factors that accelerate aging.

Treatment Duration 60–75 minutes per session
Aftercare Resume routine immediately. Avoid harsh exfoliants for 48 hours. SPF recommended.
