What are Chemical Peels?

Chemical peels use medical-grade solutions to exfoliate damaged outer layers of skin, revealing fresher, healthier skin beneath while stimulating collagen production.

Different peel strengths address different concerns. Superficial peels gently exfoliate and brighten with no downtime. Medium-depth peels penetrate further to address pigmentation, sun damage, and deeper texture concerns.

The result is smoother texture, more even tone, reduced hyperpigmentation, and a visible improvement in overall skin quality. Peels are customizable and can be combined with other treatments.

⏱ Duration 30–45 min
✨ Results 3–14 days progressive
🌿 Suitable For Most skin types
🔄 Sessions 3–6 sessions, 2–4 weeks apart

Key Benefits

✦

Texture Improvement

Chemical exfoliation removes the outer layer of damaged, irregular skin cells, revealing a smoother, more refined surface with improved texture and softness.

✦

Pigmentation Correction

Melasma, sunspots, and post-inflammatory hyperpigmentation respond well to targeted chemical peels, with visible lightening over a treatment series.

✦

Collagen Stimulation

Medium-depth peels penetrate to the dermis, triggering a wound-healing response that stimulates collagen production for longer-term structural improvement.

✦

Acne Management

Chemical peels exfoliate clogged pores, reduce surface bacteria, and regulate oil production—making them effective for both active acne and acne scarring.

The Procedure

01

Cleanse & Prepare

The skin is thoroughly cleansed and degreased to ensure even penetration of the peel solution. Eye protection is applied.

02

Peel Application

The chemical solution is carefully applied to the skin. You may feel a warm or tingling sensation. The practitioner monitors the skin’s response closely.

03

Neutralize & Soothe

The peel is neutralized at the appropriate time. A cooling mask and soothing products are applied to calm the skin and support the healing process.

Treatment Duration 30–45 min per session
Aftercare No picking or peeling flakes. SPF mandatory for 2 weeks. No active skincare for 5–7 days. Gentle cleansing only.
