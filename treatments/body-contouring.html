What is Body Contouring?

Body contouring uses advanced technologies—cryolipolysis and radiofrequency—to reduce targeted fat deposits and tighten skin without surgery.

Cryolipolysis uses controlled cooling to crystallize and eliminate fat cells in specific areas. Radiofrequency energy heats the dermis to contract collagen fibers and stimulate new collagen production for skin tightening.

These technologies allow us to sculpt the body without incisions, anesthesia, or downtime. Results develop progressively as the body processes the treated fat cells over several weeks.

⏱ Duration 35–60 min per area
✨ Results 4–12 weeks progressive
🌿 Suitable For Near ideal body weight
🔄 Sessions 1–3 sessions per area

Key Benefits

✦

Targeted Fat Reduction

Cryolipolysis precisely targets stubborn fat deposits that resist diet and exercise—belly, flanks, thighs, arms—and eliminates fat cells permanently.

✦

Skin Tightening

Radiofrequency energy heats the deep dermis, contracting existing collagen and stimulating new collagen production for firmer, tighter skin.

✦

No Surgery, No Scars

The treatment is completely non-invasive. No incisions, no anesthesia, no scars, and no downtime. You can return to work and daily activities immediately.

✦

Permanent Fat Elimination

Treated fat cells are crystallized and naturally eliminated by the body over several weeks. The reduction is permanent—those fat cells do not grow back.

The Procedure

01

Assess & Plan

A full body assessment identifies the target areas. Treatment plans are customized based on your goals, body composition, and the specific technologies best suited for you.

02

Apply Technology

Depending on the area and goal, either cryolipolysis cooling applicators or radiofrequency handpieces are applied. The treatment is comfortable—you can read, work, or relax.

03

Post-Treatment Massage

A brief manual massage of the treated area enhances the elimination of treated fat cells. You can resume all normal activities immediately afterward.

Treatment Duration 35–60 min per area per session
Aftercare Stay hydrated. Gentle massage of treated area encouraged. Healthy diet and exercise support optimal results.
