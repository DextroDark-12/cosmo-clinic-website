What is RF Skin Tightening?

RF Skin Tightening uses controlled radiofrequency energy to heat the deep layers of the skin, contracting existing collagen and stimulating the production of new collagen for firmer, tighter skin.

As we age, collagen production slows and existing collagen fibers weaken, leading to skin laxity. RF energy penetrates deep into the dermis, heating tissue to precisely controlled temperatures that cause immediate collagen contraction and initiate a long-term healing response.

The treatment is non-invasive, requires no downtime, and delivers both immediate visible tightening and progressive improvement over several months as new collagen forms.

⏱ Duration 30–60 min per area
✨ Results Immediate + progressive over 3 months
🌿 Suitable For Mild to moderate laxity
🔄 Sessions 3–6 sessions, monthly

Key Benefits

✦

Immediate Tightening

Collagen fibers contract upon heating, producing visible tightening and lifting immediately after the treatment. The effect continues to improve over months.

✦

Non-Invasive Lifting

RF technology lifts and firms without incisions, anesthesia, or recovery time. Jawline, cheeks, neck, and brows appear more defined and lifted.

✦

Long-Term Collagen Remodeling

The thermal injury triggers a prolonged wound-healing response that continues to produce new collagen for 3–6 months after the final session.

✦

Versatile Application

RF Skin Tightening can be used on the face, neck, abdomen, arms, thighs, and knees—anywhere skin laxity is a concern. Safe for all skin types.

The Procedure

01

Cleanse & Mark

The treatment area is cleansed and marked with a grid pattern to ensure even, consistent coverage. A coupling gel is applied.

02

RF Energy Delivery

The RF handpiece is moved over the treatment area, delivering controlled thermal energy to the deep dermis. You will feel a warm, soothing sensation.

03

Cool & Hydrate

The skin is cooled and a hydrating serum is applied. There is no downtime, and you can resume normal activities immediately.

Treatment Duration 30–60 min per area per session
Aftercare Stay hydrated. SPF mandatory for facial treatments. Avoid intense heat for 24 hours. Results improve progressively over several months.
