What is the Carbon Facial?

The Carbon Facial is a laser-activated treatment that deeply cleanses pores, exfoliates dead skin, and evens skin tone—all in under an hour with no downtime.

A thin layer of medical-grade carbon solution is applied to the face. As the carbon absorbs impurities, a Q-switched laser gently removes it, carrying away dead skin cells and debris while stimulating collagen production beneath.

The result is a deep clean that traditional facials cannot achieve, combined with a noticeable brightening effect. Ideal for oily, congested, or dull skin looking for a transformative yet gentle refresh.

⏱ Duration 45–60 min
✨ Results Immediate brightness
🌿 Suitable For Oily & congested skin
🔄 Sessions 4–6 monthly sessions

Key Benefits

✦

Deep Pore Cleansing

Carbon absorbs excess oil and impurities from deep within pores, then is lifted away by the laser for a level of cleanliness unattainable through manual extraction.

✦

Instant Brightening

Removal of the outer layer of dead skin cells reveals a fresher, more luminous complexion. Most clients notice visible radiance immediately after the first session.

✦

Texture Refinement

The laser’s gentle resurfacing action smooths uneven texture, reduces the appearance of enlarged pores, and softens shallow scarring over a treatment series.

✦

Oil Regulation

By decongesting pores and reducing surface bacteria, the treatment helps regulate sebum production, leading to fewer breakouts and less shine between sessions.

The Procedure

01

Cleanse & Apply Carbon

The skin is thoroughly cleansed, then a medical-grade carbon lotion is applied evenly across the face. The carbon penetrates pores, binding to oil, dirt, and dead skin cells.

02

Laser Activation

The Q-switched Nd:YAG laser is passed over the skin. The laser energy is absorbed by the carbon particles, causing them to vaporize and gently lift away impurities and dead skin cells.

03

Soothe & Protect

A calming, hydrating mask is applied to soothe the skin, followed by SPF protection. The skin is immediately fresher, cleaner, and more radiant.

Treatment Duration 45–60 min per session
Aftercare Avoid direct sun exposure for 24 hours. No harsh exfoliants for 48 hours. SPF mandatory.
