What is Profhilo?

Profhilo is a bio-remodeling injectable that stimulates collagen and elastin production to improve skin laxity, hydration, and overall quality.

Unlike traditional fillers that add volume, Profhilo works by stimulating your skin’s own regenerative processes. It contains a high concentration of hyaluronic acid that spreads evenly throughout the tissue, triggering fibroblasts to produce new collagen and elastin.

It is injected using a specific five-point technique per side of the face. The result is not volume, but rather a visible improvement in skin firmness, lift, and hydration.

⏱ Duration 30 min per session
✨ Results 2–4 weeks
🌿 Suitable For Aging & laxity concerns
🔄 Sessions 2 sessions, 1 month apart

Key Benefits

✦

Collagen Stimulation

Profhilo triggers neocollagenesis and neoelastogenesis—the production of new collagen and elastin fibers—improving skin structure from within.

✦

Visible Lifting Effect

By improving skin firmness and density, Profhilo creates a subtle lifting effect. The jawline, cheeks, and brow area appear more defined without added volume.

✦

Deep Hydration

The high-concentration hyaluronic acid distributes evenly through the tissue, providing sustained hydration that improves skin quality, texture, and radiance.

✦

Natural, Gradual Results

Results develop gradually over several weeks as your skin rebuilds collagen. The improvement is natural and progressive—you look like a better version of yourself.

The Procedure

01

Assess Tissue Quality

A full facial assessment evaluates skin laxity, volume distribution, and tissue quality. The five injection points per side are identified based on your facial anatomy.

02

Precision Injection

Using the specific BAP technique, five precise injections are made per side of the face. The product disperses evenly through the tissue, stimulating the regenerative response.

03

Follow-Up Session

A second session is scheduled 4 weeks after the first. This ensures optimal collagen stimulation. Results continue to improve over the following months.

Treatment Duration 30 min per session per session
Aftercare Avoid massaging the treated area for 24 hours. No intense heat exposure for 48 hours. Stay hydrated.
