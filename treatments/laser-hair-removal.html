What is Laser Hair Removal?

Laser hair removal uses concentrated light energy to target and disable hair follicles, resulting in permanent reduction of unwanted hair growth.

The diode laser emits a specific wavelength that is absorbed by melanin in the hair follicle. The heat energy disables the follicle’s ability to produce new hair without damaging the surrounding skin.

It is one of the most popular medical aesthetic treatments worldwide, offering freedom from the cycle of shaving, waxing, and plucking. Most patients achieve significant reduction after a full treatment series.

⏱ Duration 15–45 min per area
✨ Results Progressive over sessions
🌿 Suitable For Light to medium skin tones
🔄 Sessions 6–8 sessions, 4–6 weeks apart

Key Benefits

✦

Permanent Reduction

Clinical studies show 80–90% hair reduction after a full treatment series. The results are long-lasting and dramatically reduce the need for temporary hair removal.

✦

Speed & Convenience

Each session is quick—small areas take minutes, larger areas under an hour. No more daily shaving or monthly waxing appointments.

✦

Precision & Safety

The diode laser selectively targets dark hair while leaving surrounding skin unharmed. Advanced cooling technology ensures comfort and safety throughout the treatment.

✦

Ingrown Hair Prevention

By eliminating the hair follicle, laser treatment permanently resolves ingrown hairs, razor bumps, and the irritation associated with shaving and waxing.

The Procedure

01

Prepare & Protect

The area is cleansed and shaved. Protective eyewear is provided. A cooling gel or device is applied to protect the skin and enhance comfort.

02

Laser Application

The diode laser handpiece is passed over the treatment area, delivering precise pulses of light energy. Each pulse targets multiple hair follicles simultaneously.

03

Cool & Calm

A cooling treatment is applied to soothe the skin. You may experience mild redness for a few hours. Results develop as treated hairs shed over the following 1–3 weeks.

Treatment Duration 15–45 min per area per session
Aftercare Avoid sun exposure for 2 weeks. No waxing or plucking between sessions. Shaving is permitted. SPF mandatory on treated areas.

Frequently Asked Questions

Most patients describe the sensation as a warm snap with each pulse, similar to a rubber band lightly tapping the skin. Modern cooling technology keeps the skin comfortable throughout.

We recommend 6–8 sessions spaced 4–6 weeks apart. Hair grows in cycles, and multiple sessions are needed to catch all follicles in their active growth phase.

The FDA term is ‘permanent reduction.’ Most patients experience 80–90% reduction after a full series. Some maintenance sessions may be needed annually.

Modern diode lasers can safely treat a wide range of skin types. The treatment works best on darker hair against lighter skin. A consultation will determine your candidacy.

Any area with unwanted hair can be treated: face, underarms, bikini, legs, arms, back, chest, and abdomen. We will create a personalized plan for your needs.
