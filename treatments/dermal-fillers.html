What are Dermal Fillers?

Dermal fillers are injectable hyaluronic acid gels that restore lost volume, soften deep folds, and enhance facial contours with subtle, natural-looking results.

As we age, our skin loses hyaluronic acid, collagen, and elastin—the building blocks that keep it plump and supported. Dermal fillers replenish this lost volume, lifting and smoothing areas that have begun to sag or hollow.

Our clinic uses premium hyaluronic acid fillers that integrate naturally into the tissue. Results are immediate and can last anywhere from 6 to 18 months depending on the area treated and the product used.

⏱ Duration 30–45 min
✨ Results Immediate
🌿 Suitable For Aging & volume loss concerns
🔄 Sessions 1–2 initial, touch-ups at 6–12 mo

Key Benefits

✦

Immediate Volume Restoration

Lost facial volume is restored in a single session. Cheeks regain lift, lips regain definition, and deep folds soften—visible before you leave the clinic.

✦

Natural-Looking Results

Premium hyaluronic acid formulations integrate naturally with your tissue, creating soft, expressive results that enhance your features rather than changing them.

✦

Customizable Treatment

Each treatment is tailored to your facial anatomy and goals. We can address specific areas—lips, cheeks, jawline, tear troughs—with precision and artistry.

✦

Gradual & Reversible

Results are not permanent, allowing your look to evolve over time. Hyaluronic acid fillers can be dissolved if needed, providing flexibility and peace of mind.

The Procedure

01

Assess & Map

A detailed facial assessment identifies areas of volume loss and structural imbalance. We map injection points to ensure symmetrical, harmonious results.

02

Precision Injection

Using ultra-fine needles or cannulas, the filler is injected with precision into the targeted areas. A topical anesthetic ensures comfort throughout.

03

Mold & Assess

The practitioner gently molds the filler to ensure smooth integration. You assess the results in real time, and adjustments are made as needed.

Treatment Duration 30–45 min per session
Aftercare Avoid pressure on treated areas for 24 hours. No intense exercise for 48 hours. Mild swelling is normal.
