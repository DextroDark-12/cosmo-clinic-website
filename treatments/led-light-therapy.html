What is LED Light Therapy?

LED Light Therapy uses specific wavelengths of light to stimulate cellular activity, reduce inflammation, and accelerate the skin’s natural healing processes.

Different wavelengths target different skin concerns. Red light penetrates deep into the dermis, stimulating collagen production and reducing inflammation. Blue light targets surface bacteria, making it effective for acne.

The treatment is completely non-invasive, painless, and requires no downtime. It is often used as a standalone treatment or combined with other procedures to enhance healing and results.

⏱ Duration 20–30 min
✨ Results Cumulative over sessions
🌿 Suitable For All skin types
🔄 Sessions 6–12 sessions, weekly

Key Benefits

✦

Collagen Stimulation

Red and near-infrared light penetrate deep into the skin, stimulating fibroblast activity and increasing collagen production for improved firmness and texture.

✦

Inflammation Reduction

Specific wavelengths modulate inflammatory responses, reducing redness, calming irritation, and supporting the healing of sensitive or compromised skin.

✦

Acne Management

Blue light targets P. acnes bacteria on the skin’s surface, reducing active breakouts and preventing future flare-ups without harsh chemicals or irritation.

✦

Enhanced Healing

LED therapy accelerates tissue repair, making it an excellent post-procedure treatment after microneedling, peels, or laser treatments to speed recovery.

The Procedure

01

Cleanse & Prepare

The skin is thoroughly cleansed to remove any products, oils, or impurities that could interfere with light penetration. Eye protection is applied.

02

Light Application

The LED panel is positioned at the prescribed distance from your face. You relax while the specific wavelength delivers therapeutic energy for 20–30 minutes.

03

Post-Treatment Care

There is no downtime. You can resume normal activities immediately. SPF is recommended, and the skin will be more receptive to topical products afterward.

Treatment Duration 20–30 min per session
Aftercare No special aftercare required. SPF recommended. Skin may be more receptive to products post-treatment.
