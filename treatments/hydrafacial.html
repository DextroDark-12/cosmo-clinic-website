What is HydraFacial?

The HydraFacial is a medical-grade hydradermabrasion treatment that simultaneously cleanses, exfoliates, extracts, and infuses skin with intensive serums.

Using patented vortex technology, it removes impurities while delivering antioxidant-rich boosters tailored to your skin's unique needs. Unlike traditional facials, HydraFacial delivers visible results without irritation or downtime.

⏱ Duration 45–60 min
✨ Results Immediate
🌿 Suitable For All skin types
🔄 Maintenance Monthly

Key Benefits

✦

Instant Radiance

Walk out with skin that genuinely glows. Dead cells and dulling debris swept away, revealing the fresh layer beneath.

✦

Deep Cleansing

Vortex suction clears pores of dirt, oil, and debris that daily cleansing misses. Your skin breathes cleaner than it has in months.

✦

Hydration Boost

Antioxidant-rich serums penetrate deep into the skin, delivering lasting hydration that topical products alone cannot achieve.

✦

Gentle Exfoliation

Medical-grade exfoliation removes the outer layer of dead skin without harsh abrasives or irritation—safe for sensitive skin.

The Procedure

01

Cleanse & Exfoliate

A gentle yet thorough cleanse, followed by mild exfoliation to remove the outer layer of dead skin cells. This reveals a fresher surface and prepares your skin for deeper treatment.

02

Extract & Hydrate

Patented vortex suction painlessly extracts impurities from deep within your pores. Simultaneously, skin-boosting antioxidants and hyaluronic acid are infused to replenish and restore.

03

Protect & Fuse

A final application of protective serums—peptides, antioxidants, and collagen-boosting agents—seals your skin with a customized cocktail designed for your specific concerns.

Treatment Duration 45–60 minutes per session
Aftercare Resume normal routine immediately. Avoid retinoids for 48 hours.
