What is Botox?

Botox is a purified neurotoxin that temporarily relaxes the muscles responsible for dynamic wrinkles—those caused by repeated facial expressions like frowning, squinting, and smiling.

When injected in precise, small amounts, Botox blocks nerve signals to specific facial muscles, allowing them to relax. This softens existing lines and prevents the deepening of new ones, all while preserving natural facial expression.

The treatment is quick, takes about 15 minutes, and requires no downtime. Results appear gradually over 3–7 days and typically last 3–4 months.

⏱ Duration 15–20 min
✨ Results 3–7 days
🌿 Suitable For All skin types
🔄 Sessions Every 3–4 months

Key Benefits

✦

Wrinkle Smoothing

Dynamic wrinkles on the forehead, between the brows, and around the eyes are visibly softened. The skin looks smoother and more rested without losing natural expression.

✦

Preventive Benefits

Regular treatment prevents dynamic wrinkles from becoming static, etched-in lines. Many patients start Botox in their late 20s and 30s as a preventive measure.

✦

Quick & Convenient

The injection process takes less than 20 minutes with no downtime. You can return to work and daily activities immediately after treatment.

✦

Subtle, Natural Results

When administered by an experienced practitioner, results are subtle and natural. You will look refreshed—not frozen or expressionless.

The Procedure

01

Assess Expression Patterns

The practitioner assesses your facial muscle activity and identifies the specific areas contributing to dynamic wrinkles. A customized treatment plan is created.

02

Micro-Injections

Using ultra-fine needles, precise micro-doses of Botox are injected into the targeted muscles. The process is quick and well-tolerated, with minimal discomfort.

03

Post-Treatment Care

Results develop over the next 3–7 days as the muscles gradually relax. A follow-up at two weeks allows us to assess results and make any fine adjustments.

Treatment Duration 15–20 min per session
Aftercare Avoid rubbing the treated area for 24 hours. No strenuous exercise for 24 hours. Results visible within 3–7 days.
