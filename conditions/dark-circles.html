Understanding Your Skin

What Causes Dark Circles?

The under-eye area is one of the thinnest and most sensitive regions of the face, making it particularly vulnerable to volume loss and shadowing.

Common Signs

  • Hollow or sunken appearance
  • Blue or purple-tinted shadows
  • Tired or aged expression
  • Loss of facial volume

Root Causes

  • Thin under-eye skin revealing blood vessels
  • Volume loss in the tear trough area
  • Genetic predisposition
  • Natural aging process

After Your Treatment

Recovery & Aftercare

Proper aftercare ensures the best possible outcome for your eye area treatment.

Protect from Sun

Apply SPF 50+ around the eyes daily. UV exposure worsens pigmentation and skin damage.

Gentle Skincare

Use fragrance-free eye creams. Avoid harsh actives near the treated area for one week.

Stay Hydrated

Drink plenty of water and maintain a healthy sleep schedule to support skin recovery.

Questions

Frequently Asked

How many treatments will I need? +

Most patients see significant improvement after 2-3 Profhilo sessions, spaced 4 weeks apart. HydraFacial can be performed monthly for maintenance.

Is there any downtime? +

Profhilo may cause mild swelling or bruising at injection sites, usually resolving within 24-48 hours. HydraFacial has no downtime.

When will I see results? +

Initial improvements are visible within days, with full results developing over 4-6 weeks as collagen production increases.
