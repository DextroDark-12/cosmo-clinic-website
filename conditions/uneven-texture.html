Understanding Your Skin

What Causes Uneven Texture?

Uneven skin texture develops from multiple factors, but can be effectively treated with the right combination of resurfacing techniques.

Common Signs

  • Rough or uneven surface
  • Bumpy or irregular texture
  • Enlarged or visible pores
  • Skin congestion and buildup

Root Causes

  • Dead skin cell accumulation
  • Sun damage and photoaging
  • Natural aging process
  • Pore congestion and blockages

After Your Treatment

Recovery & Aftercare

Proper aftercare maximises your resurfacing results and protects new skin.

Avoid Sun Exposure

Use SPF 50+ daily. New skin is particularly vulnerable to UV damage and pigmentation.

Keep Skin Hydrated

Use a gentle moisturiser to support skin healing. Avoid harsh or active ingredients for one week.

Be Patient

Texture improvements develop gradually. Full results typically appear 4-6 weeks after treatment.

Questions

Frequently Asked

Which treatment is best for my skin? +

During consultation, we'll assess your skin and recommend the optimal treatment or combination. Many patients benefit from a customised approach using multiple techniques.

How long is the recovery period? +

Recovery varies by treatment. Carbon Facial has no downtime, while Microneedling and Chemical Peels may require 3-7 days of gentle care.

When will I see improvements? +

Most patients notice skin smoothness within days, with significant texture improvements developing over 4-8 weeks as collagen remodels.
