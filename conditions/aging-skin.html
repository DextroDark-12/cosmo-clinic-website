Understanding Your Skin

Your Skin's Natural Evolution

Aging is a beautiful part of life's journey, but we understand the desire to maintain healthy, vibrant skin at every stage. Whether you're noticing the first fine lines or seeking to restore more significant changes, our approach adapts to your unique biology and aesthetic goals.

Our treatments work with your skin's natural processes, stimulating collagen production, improving elasticity, and addressing specific concerns like sun damage and uneven texture. We combine advanced technology with medical expertise to deliver results that look entirely natural.

Common Signs

  • Fine lines around eyes and mouth
  • Decreased skin firmness and elasticity
  • Age spots and uneven pigmentation
  • Dull, dehydrated appearance

Root Causes

  • Natural collagen and elastin decline
  • Cumulative sun exposure damage
  • Free radical damage and oxidative stress
  • Lifestyle factors and hormonal changes

After Your Treatment

Recovery & Aftercare

Proper aftercare is the most important part of achieving and maintaining your results.

Sun Protection is Essential

Apply SPF 50+ daily without exception. UV exposure accelerates aging and can compromise treatment results. Reapply every 2 hours during sun exposure.

Hydration Inside and Out

Drink at least 2 litres of water daily and maintain a consistent moisturising routine. Hydrated skin responds better to treatments and heals faster.

Patience with Results

Collagen regeneration takes time. Expect to see progressive improvements over 3-6 months as your skin naturally rebuilds and restores its youthful structure.

Questions

Frequently Asked

At what age should I start anti-aging treatments? +

There's no universal answer—prevention is always more effective than correction. Many patients begin preventive treatments in their late twenties or early thirties with antioxidant facials and sun protection. However, treatments can be highly effective at any age. Your first consultation will help us create a timeline that makes sense for your skin's current condition and your goals.

How many treatments will I need to see results? +

This depends entirely on your chosen treatments and the severity of your concerns. Some patients see improvements after a single session, while most protocols recommend a course of 3-6 treatments for optimal results. We'll provide a detailed treatment plan during your consultation, including expected timelines and maintenance requirements.

Are anti-aging treatments safe for all skin types? +

Yes, our treatments are suitable for all skin types and tones. During your consultation, we assess your skin's unique characteristics and any specific considerations. Certain treatments, like Profhilo and RF skin tightening, are particularly versatile and effective across diverse skin types, while others can be adjusted in intensity to match your skin's response.

What's the difference between non-surgical and surgical anti-aging? +

Our non-surgical treatments focus on stimulating natural skin processes—collagen production, cellular renewal, and tissue repair—delivering gradual, natural-looking improvements without surgery or significant downtime. While surgical options provide more dramatic lifting, our advanced technologies can achieve remarkable rejuvenation that many patients find preferable for its subtlety and convenience.
