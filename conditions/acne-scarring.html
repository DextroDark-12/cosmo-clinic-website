Understanding Your Skin

From Breakouts to Clear Skin

Acne affects millions of people at any age, and its impact extends far beyond the physical. Whether you're experiencing active breakouts or living with the aftermath of past acne, we understand how this condition affects your confidence and quality of life.

Our approach combines medical expertise with advanced treatment technologies to address both the immediate concerns of active acne and the long-term goal of achieving clear, smooth skin. We treat every patient as an individual, recognising that acne triggers and responses vary significantly.

Common Signs

  • Active breakouts and blemishes
  • Post-inflammatory hyperpigmentation
  • Rolling, boxcar, or icepick scars
  • Enlarged pores and uneven texture

Root Causes

  • Excess sebum production
  • Cutibacterium acnes bacteria
  • Chronic inflammation and congestion
  • Unhealthy picking and touching habits

After Your Treatment

Recovery & Aftercare

Proper aftercare is the most important part of achieving and maintaining your results.

Keep Hands Off

Resist touching, picking, or squeezing blemishes. This prolongs healing, increases infection risk, and worsens scarring. Let treatments do the work.

Non-Comedogenic Products Only

Switch to oil-free, non-comedogenic skincare and makeup. Ingredients that clog pores undermine your treatment progress and can trigger new breakouts.

Consistency is Key

Acne treatment requires patience and commitment. Follow your prescribed at-home routine alongside clinic treatments for cumulative improvements over 8-12 weeks.

Questions

Frequently Asked

Will acne treatments work on all skin types? +

Absolutely. Our treatments are customised for all skin types and tones. During consultation, we assess your specific skin characteristics and any considerations such as darker skin's tendency toward hyperpigmentation. We adjust peel depths, treatment intensities, and aftercare protocols accordingly to ensure safety and efficacy for every patient.

How long does it take to see results from acne treatments? +

Active acne improvements often appear within 4-6 weeks of starting treatment. Scar reduction is more gradual—most patients see meaningful texture improvements at 3-4 months, with continued enhancement over 6-12 months as collagen remodelling progresses. We'll set realistic expectations based on your specific concerns during consultation.

Can acne come back after treatment? +

Acne is a chronic condition for many people, and while we can achieve significant clearing, maintenance is often needed. The good news is that our treatments improve skin quality and reduce the frequency and severity of breakouts long-term. We'll teach you to recognise triggers and maintain results with appropriate skincare and periodic treatments.

What's the difference between treating active acne versus scars? +

Active acne treatment focuses on controlling bacteria, reducing oil production, and clearing congestion. Scar treatment, however, aims to rebuild collagen and remodel tissue to smooth skin texture. Many patients benefit from addressing both simultaneously, and we'll design a phased plan that prioritises active breakouts first while beginning scar work when appropriate.
