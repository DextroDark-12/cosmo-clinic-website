Understanding Your Needs

Scalp Wellness First

Hair loss affects both men and women and carries emotional weight that extends far beyond aesthetics. We approach every case with sensitivity, recognising that this is a personal journey with unique triggers and treatment considerations for each individual.

Our philosophy centres on scalp wellness—creating the optimal environment for hair follicles to function and potentially regenerate. While results vary based on the underlying cause and individual factors, many patients experience meaningful improvement through our targeted treatment approaches.

Common Signs

  • Progressive hair thinning
  • Visible scalp through hair
  • Reduced hair volume and density
  • Increased daily hair shedding

Contributing Factors

  • Genetic predisposition
  • Hormonal changes and imbalances
  • Stress and lifestyle factors
  • Nutritional deficiencies

Maximising Results

Treatment effectiveness extends beyond the clinic. Your daily habits significantly influence outcomes.

Consistent Treatment Schedule

Hair restoration requires regular, consistent sessions—typically weekly treatments for several months initially. Skipping appointments or stopping prematurely will limit results. Commit to the recommended protocol.

Nutrition Matters

Hair follicles require adequate nutrients. Ensure sufficient protein intake and consider supplements if deficient in iron, vitamin D, zinc, or B vitamins. A balanced diet supports treatment effectiveness.

Stress Management

Chronic stress exacerbates hair loss. Incorporate stress-reduction practices—exercise, adequate sleep, mindfulness—into your routine. Stress hormones directly impact follicle function.

Questions

Frequently Asked

Will hair loss treatments work for me? +

This depends on the cause and extent of your hair loss, among other individual factors. During your consultation, we assess your scalp condition, discuss your medical history, and identify likely contributing factors. We set realistic expectations based on this assessment—some patients see significant improvement, while others see more modest results. The consultation itself provides clarity on your specific situation.

How long before I see results from hair restoration treatments? +

Hair growth follows a slow process—visible changes typically require 3-6 months of consistent treatment. Some patients notice reduced shedding earlier, at 4-8 weeks, but meaningful cosmetic improvement comes with sustained follicle stimulation over months. We'll monitor your progress and adjust protocols as needed.

Is hair loss treatment permanent? +

Hair loss is often an ongoing condition, particularly when genetic or hormonal factors are involved. Our treatments can slow progression, improve hair quality, and stimulate growth—but maintenance is typically needed. Think of it as managing a chronic condition rather than a one-time cure. We'll discuss long-term maintenance strategies during your consultation.

What's the difference between your approach and medications like finasteride? +

Our treatments work differently—they stimulate follicles directly through light energy and controlled micro-injury. Unlike medications, there's no systemic side effect profile. However, medications can be highly effective for androgenetic alopecia and are often discussed as part of a comprehensive approach. During consultation, we'll discuss whether combining treatments—including medical options—makes sense for your situation.
