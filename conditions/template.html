After Your Treatment

Recovery & Aftercare

Proper aftercare is the most important part of achieving and maintaining your results.

Avoid Sun Exposure

Use SPF 50+ daily for at least 2–4 weeks post-treatment to protect healing skin.

Gentle Skincare Only

Stick to a mild cleanser and fragrance-free moisturiser. No active acids for 7 days.

Stay Hydrated

Drink plenty of water. Your skin needs internal hydration and room to breathe.
