Understanding Your Skin

Understanding Sensitive Skin

Sensitive skin requires a thoughtful approach. Our treatments are designed to soothe and strengthen without causing further irritation.

Common Signs

  • Persistent redness
  • Heightened skin reactivity
  • Burning or stinging sensation
  • Frequent flushing

Root Causes

  • Compromised skin barrier
  • Genetic predisposition
  • Environmental triggers
  • Stress and lifestyle factors

After Your Treatment

Recovery & Aftercare

Supporting your sensitive skin with the right aftercare routine is essential.

Use Gentle Products

Stick to fragrance-free, hypoallergenic skincare. Avoid acids and retinoids for 48 hours.

Sun Protection

Apply a mineral-based SPF 50+ daily. Physical sunscreen is less likely to irritate sensitive skin.

Stay Consistent

Consistency is key. Follow your recommended skincare routine for lasting results.
