FAQ

Answers to common patient questions

What should I expect during my first visit?

Your first visit will include a thorough consultation. We'll discuss your skin concerns, goals, and medical history.

Are the treatments painful?

Most treatments are well-tolerated with minimal discomfort. We offer numbing creams for sensitive procedures.

How many treatments will I need?

This depends on your specific concerns. Some patients see results after one treatment, others may need 4-6 sessions.

Do you offer consultations?

Yes! We offer free initial consultations to discuss your skin goals and recommend treatments.
